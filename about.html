About Me

I am a 23 year old woman who has worked in multiple industries, whether that be food, utility, or retail. What all have in common is customer service! While I love customer service and have excelled in that skill, it wasn't my calling. The tech world has always been a environment I wanted to be apart of, however the insecurity of not having that degree and being in a male dominant world was worrisome. However, I started doing my research and found women like me who put in the work and learned to code all on their own and have excelled! With the work I've been putting in my goal within the year is to find myself working in the tech world.

You can find some of my recent work on Github! I started to learn to code in October of 2023 with SheCodes and it has been the best decision I have ever made! I love feeling creative and having control over my work and creating something I can be proud of. It isn't an easy learning journey but with the hours put in, it becomes rewarding to be able to learn new things and to be able to apply it to developing my own projects! I'm looking to be a front-end developer and to help create something beautiful that consumers will enjoy.
